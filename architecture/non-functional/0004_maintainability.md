# ADR 0004: Maintainability

## Status
Proposed | ~~Accepted~~ | ~~Superseded~~

## Context

Business requirement for project to be simple and low maintanance.
The product being developed is a simple application.

Two full time engineers are responsible for the full stack application, infrastructure and support.
A three year support window is specified.

## Decision

Negotiate maintanace Vs performance as sub 1s interactivity over mobile networks may require compromise.

Use managed services where possible.
Use SaaS offerings where possible.

Will not be technology evangelists or first adopters as on 3 year window and limited team.

Will need to provide robust tools to engineers for maintanance tasks.

## Considerations

As this is a full stack application it's most likely developer time will be spent on UI improvements and app bug fixes.
Plus technical support issues that arise (VIP locks themselves out of account).
Therefore, limited time for infrastructure issues.
Futhermore, with only 2 engineers it's highly likely they will be specialised in application development and not infrastructure.
Need to provide tools for them to do thier maintainance.
