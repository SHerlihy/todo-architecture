# ADR 0005: SAR Accessibility

## Status
Proposed | ~~Accepted~~ | ~~Superseded~~

## Context

Reasonable adjustments are required to provide people with SAR information.
The larger the company the greater the expectation to provide more diverse information types (i.e. braille prints, word documents).

A company can mostly stick to providing digital documents only.
However, non-sighted people may struggle with some file formats.

## Decision

Recommend operating the software as a separate business so it does not fall into the expectation of larger companies.
PII data will be provided in .html files to service sighted and non-sighted people as this works well with screen readers.

## Considerations

Low maintainance is specified in the business requirements and maintaining multiple media formats for accessibility reduces maintainability.
