Business Requirements Document (BRD)Project Name: TaskBuddy (Internal Task Tracker)Version: 1.0Date: July 9, 2026Author: Sam Lee, Operations Manager1. Executive SummaryOur agency currently uses email and scattered chat threads to assign tasks, resulting in 20% of project deadlines being missed due to poor visibility. TaskBuddy will be a lightweight, single-page web application that allows team leads to assign tasks, track statuses in a simple visual board, and send automated morning summaries to team members.2. Business ObjectivesEliminate Dropped Tasks: Reduce missed internal project deadlines to less than 2% within 30 days of rollout.Centralize Visibility: Replace email-based task tracking with a single source of truth that takes less than 5 minutes a day for employees to update.3. Scope of the ProjectIn-ScopeA responsive web-based Kanban board view (To Do, In Progress, Done).Email notifications for task assignments and daily morning summaries.Simple user profiles for internal staff members.Out-of-ScopeClient-facing portals or external user access.Time-tracking timers or billing/invoicing integrations.Native iOS or Android mobile apps (web-browser version only).4.
